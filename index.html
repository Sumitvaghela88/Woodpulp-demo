WHY WOOD PULP?

  • Quality Assurance by state of the art Manufacturing facility.
  • In House Glue preparation system for better quality & consistency.
  • High IB value leads to better, “Screw holding strength”.
  • Made from 100% fresh wood, “Saves tooling cost.”
  • A pure melamine coating leads to “moisture scratch & Fade” resistant lamination property.
  • HPL like high abrasion resistant surface finish.

How We Work

Log Storage
Convert into Chips and Chips to Flakes.
Drying the flakes to the desired moisture level.
Screening the dried flakes into Core and Face.
Gluing them with high quality resin.
Preparing the Matt and Hot pressing it for final curing.
Sanding and Sizing the cured panel.
